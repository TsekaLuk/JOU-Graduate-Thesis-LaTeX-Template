\JOUFrontmatterEnglishTitle{Abstract}

{\rmfamily\zihao{-4}
Cyclonic static micro-bubble flotation is a novel column separation method with
independent intellectual property rights in China. Its characteristic cyclonic field
structure and successful application in coal preparation provide a solid basis for the
extension of flotation column technology to broader mineral separation scenarios.

\vspace{0.8\baselineskip}

This thesis focuses on the field structure, particle force balance and separation
behavior inside the flotation cyclone. The study first reviews the development
background and current research status of the equipment, then organizes the
research objectives and technical route. Based on fluid analysis, experimental
observation and operating-condition comparison, the effects of circulating pulp
rate, feed rate and column back pressure on the separation performance are
discussed.

\vspace{0.8\baselineskip}

The results provide a compact reference for structure optimization, parameter
tuning and engineering application of flotation cyclonic equipment.
}

\JOUENKeywords{flotation; cyclonic separation; separation mechanism; flotation kinetics; mineral separation}
